DeEsser

This plug-in filters/attenuates sibilants in vocal recordings in a simple yet effective way. Since the underlying process operates on the basis of a dynamic filter, other signals are weakened such as cymbals in drum recordings or other signals in a similar frequency range.

The DeEsser works, in contrast to other devices without adjustable thresholds. The plug-in constantly evaluates the input signal and recognizes signal peaks that are high above in the frequency range of the average levels. Through this comparative analysis with the wanted signal, a constant reduction of noisy sections can be adhered to, so a traditional threshold often requires adjustments for changing levels.

DeEsser Parameters

freq: Determines the frequency of the filter used for detection, and filter blocking in the signal route. Typically the sibilants in speech or singing voices are in the 6 - 8 kHz range.

Speaker symbol (pre-listen): This allows you to hear the "solo" filter frequency so you can locate noisy sections quickly and easily.

reduction: Regulates the filter attenuation in the signal route.
